Real-Life Example

A student prepares for an exam like JAMB or WAEC. The syllabus is available. The exam date is fixed. The opportunity is clear.

But preparation is inconsistent. Excuses replace discipline. Time is spent without structure.

When the result comes, failure is often blamed on timing or difficulty. But the exam did not delay. The opportunity arrived exactly when scheduled.

The real gap was not time. It was readiness.

Nothing in life delays, only our readiness does.

— Ezenwa Chidera Emmanuel
