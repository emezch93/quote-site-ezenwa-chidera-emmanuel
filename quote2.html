“Nothing in life delays, only our readiness does”
Meaning

Life does not delay opportunities. Time moves independently of human expectation. What people often interpret as delay is usually a mismatch between opportunity and readiness.

Readiness determines access, not timing.

Core Insight

Outcomes are rarely blocked by life itself. They are shaped by preparation, consistency, and the ability to act when opportunity appears.

— Ezenwa Chidera Emmanuel
