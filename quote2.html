“Nothing in life delays, only our readiness does”

Meaning

Life does not delay opportunities. Time moves independently of human expectation. What appears as delay is often a lack of readiness.

Core Insight

Outcomes are shaped more by preparation than timing. Readiness determines access to opportunity.

Philosophical View

The universe does not pause for human readiness. It continues to unfold, and individuals align with outcomes only when preparation meets opportunity.
